PANEL ON SMART CITY INNOVATION AND TRENDS

This Smartcity event event will feature a panel on Smart City Innovation and Trends. The smart city concept integrates information and communication technology, and various physical devices connected to the IoT network to optimize the efficiency of city operations and services and connect to citizens.

Panelists from the industry will explore the effects of Smart City innovation on traditional areas of SS, talk about the current Smart City trends and discuss the future of Smart City. The event will be composed of 2 sessions
